NATIONAL CAPITAL REGION: Cherry Blossoms Visits Dip



ANALYSIS: The most concentrated number of U.S. National Parks is the National Capital Region (DC, MD, VA). Many might be surprised but the Lincoln Memorial, Wolf Trap National Park for the Performing Arts, and the White House are part of the National Park Service System. I lived in this region for 13 years-the early stage of my professional career-and enjoyed the close proximity to so many parks. Each year, the tourist season kicks off at the end of March or begninng of April when the Cherry Blossoms at in full bloom for two weeks. This event typically draws millions of visitors each year. This is true in 2019 but not in 2020. As we see from the brown line, brought 6.5 million visitors in April 2019. For that same month a year later, the visits were just below 1 million, a substantial drop from the year prior. What is interesting about this graph is the large gap between the lines from March through August. While we see a decline in visits in 2019 after April, the visits in 2020 came nowhere close to the same levels in 2019. Clearly, COVID:19 restrictions were felt in the National Captial Region.
